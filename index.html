Our Story

Healthcare Built on Trust, Not Just Transactions

NexaCare Medical Centre was founded in 2012 by Dr. Farrukh Siddiqui with a clear vision: bring the standard of a top private hospital to a neighbourhood clinic that families in DHA could actually afford — and trust.

Over 12 years, we've grown from a single consultation room to a fully equipped multi-specialty centre serving over 18,000 patients. But our approach has never changed: listen first, diagnose carefully, treat with compassion.

What We Offer

Comprehensive Care Under One Roof

From your first visit to ongoing specialist care, NexaCare covers every step of your health journey with expertise and compassion.

Book a Consultation
Service 01
General Medicine & OPD

Consultations for fevers, infections, diabetes management, hypertension, and general health screening. Walk-ins welcome — appointments preferred for shorter waits.

Service 02
Diagnostics & Pathology Lab

Complete blood counts, lipid profiles, HbA1c, thyroid panels, urine & culture — processed in our on-site lab with results ready in 2–4 hours.

Service 03
Cardiology & ECG

Resting ECG, blood pressure monitoring, and full cardiac risk assessment with our on-site cardiologist. Referrals for echocardiography also arranged.

Service 04
Paediatrics & Child Health

Growth monitoring, EPI vaccinations, sick-child consultations, and developmental assessments — in a calm, child-friendly space your kids will actually feel comfortable in.
